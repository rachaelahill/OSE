Open Source Ecology is a network of farmers, engineers, and supporters. For the last two years we have been creating the Global Village Construction Set. An open source, low-cost, high performance technological platform that allows for the easy, DIY fabrication of the 50 different Industrial Machines that it takes to build a sustainable civilization with modern comforts.
